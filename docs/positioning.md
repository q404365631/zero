# Positioning

ZERO is the autonomous operating system for self-custodial onchain operations.

The first wedge is onchain perpetual markets. The broader product is a control
plane for autonomous capital operations: runtime, terminal, safety model, public
proof, and verified intelligence.

## Category

Autonomous operating system for self-custodial onchain operations.

This category is broader than "trading bot" and more precise than "AI agent."
It says what ZERO operates, who keeps control, and why the product can expand
from Hyperliquid-first execution into treasury, hedging, multi-venue routing,
and verified intelligence.

## One-liner

ZERO makes autonomous onchain operations inspectable, interruptible, and
self-custodial.

## Narrative

Crypto markets are machine-speed and always open. Human attention is not. Most
operator failure is not caused by missing another signal; it comes from
inconsistent execution, unmanaged risk drift, unclear state, and manual
intervention at the wrong time.

ZERO starts from a different premise: autonomous systems need an operating
system, not another alert feed. The runtime evaluates opportunities, rejects weak
actions, records decisions, and enforces safety policy. The terminal gives the
operator state, replay, and supervised control. The network exposes public-safe
proof. The intelligence layer turns verified behavior into a data product.

The operator keeps custody. ZERO provides the discipline layer.

## Enemy

Attention-driven onchain infrastructure:

- Interfaces that reward activity over outcomes.
- Bots that automate entry but underinvest in safety and post-entry control.
- Copy-trading feeds that cannot be audited or adapted to the operator's policy.
- Dashboards that show state without making the system safer.

## Product Principles

- Self-custody first.
- Paper mode before live mode.
- Inspectable behavior over vague claims.
- Risk-reducing actions fast, risk-increasing actions deliberate.
- Public proof without leaking secrets.
- Commercial value based on speed, scale, history, reliability, and verified
  intelligence.

## Surfaces

- ZERO Runtime: the local engine for autonomous operations.
- ZERO Terminal: the operator interface for state, diagnostics, replay, and
  supervised actions.
- ZERO Network: public profiles, leaderboards, verification badges, and proof
  packets.
- ZERO Intelligence: public snapshots plus commercial realtime APIs, history,
  cohorts, webhooks, exports, and support.

## Recommended Copy

Short GitHub description:

```text
Autonomous operating system for self-custodial onchain operations.
```

Extended description:

```text
ZERO is an open-source runtime and operator terminal for autonomous onchain
operations: paper-first execution, safety gates, local journals, public proof,
and commercial intelligence contracts.
```

What to avoid:

- "AI trading bot"
- "Guaranteed yield"
- "Hosted custody"
- "Fully trustless AI trader"
- "Set and forget"

Those phrases either shrink the category, create regulatory and trust risk, or
misrepresent the safety model.
