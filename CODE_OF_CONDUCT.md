# Code of Conduct

ZERO follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Keep technical criticism specific and actionable.
- Assume maintainers are optimizing for safety, correctness, and long-term project health.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Threats or harassment
- Sexualized language or attention
- Doxxing or publishing private information
- Sustained disruption of project work
- Encouraging unsafe real-money trading behavior

## Enforcement

Report concerns to security@getzero.dev or the maintainer contact listed in `SECURITY.md`.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or escalate reports when needed.
